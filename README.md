# NightOwl
Be honest. When did you actually sleep?
Enter your sleep and wake time — NightOwl calculates your hours, rates your schedule, and roasts you for it.
Live Demo
🔗 jasnoor-kaur1501.github.io/NightOwl
What it does

Enter sleep time, wake time, and days per week
Calculates total sleep hours and weekly sleep debt vs 8h ideal
Rates your schedule across 6 tiers from Disaster to Elite
Roasts you with a different line every time based on your pattern
Copy your roast to share
